About Our Team

A Japan-based team built for global healthcare research — bilingual execution, careful ethics, and practical insight.

Who we are

Seed Planning is a Japan-based market research & consulting firm. Our Global Medical Business Unit supports international pharmaceutical, biotech, and medical device teams who need Japan insight they can trust — without losing cultural nuance.

We run qualitative and quantitative studies, recruit hard-to-reach participants (including rare disease communities), and deliver English-ready outputs: screeners, discussion guides, transcripts, analysis, and reporting decks.
